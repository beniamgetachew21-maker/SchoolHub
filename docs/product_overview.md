## **Elevate Every Department**

Campus Hub unifies your entire campus into a single, seamless ecosystem. No more data silos, no more manual bottlenecks.

### 🏛️ **Core Institutional Management**
- **Student Information System**: Centralized student profiles with 360° views of admissions, records, and disciplinary tracking.
- **Academics Management**: Effortlessly handle gradebooks, attendance, examination scheduling, and complex timetables.
- **HR & Payroll**: Manage the full employee lifecycle—from automated recruitment and onboarding to leave management and payroll.

### 💰 **Seamless Financial Operations**
- **Finance & Fees**: Securely manage fee collection, automated invoicing, and expense tracking. Integrated with **Stripe** for seamless online payments.
- **Inventory & Procurement**: Track every asset across campus, manage vendors, and streamline purchase orders with precision.

### 🚀 **Next-Gen AI & Innovation**
- **AI-Powered Tools**: Leverage cutting-edge AI to verify birth certificates, generate custom student assessments, and draft professional job descriptions in seconds.
- **ID Card Generation**: Design and print high-quality, professional ID cards for students, staff, and visitors on-demand.
- **Customizable UI**: A flexible, theme-able interface that adapts to your institution’s branding and unique workflow needs.

### 🤝 **Community & Wellness**
- **Communication Suite**: Engage parents and students with targeted announcements, newsletters, and a robust subscriber system.
- **User Portals**: Dedicated, secure environments for Parents, Students, and Teachers to stay informed and productive.
- **Health & Wellness**: Track medical records and infirmary visits, ensuring student safety is always a priority.
- **Extracurricular & Alumni**: Manage clubs and events while keeping your graduates connected with a dedicated alumni directory and job board.
